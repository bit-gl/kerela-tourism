DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM

Kerala State

Kerala, located in the south-western part of India, is known for its lush greenery, scenic landscapes, and cultural richness. Often referred to as "God's Own Country", Kerala attracts tourists from all over the world with its:

1.Serene backwaters
2.Majestic houseboats
3.Colorful festivals like Onam
4.Ayurvedic treatments and pristine beaches

Recognized by National Geographic Traveller as one of the world's top 50 destinations of a lifetime, Kerala is truly a paradise on Earth.

Come, experience the magic of Kerala!
